The Cat Protection Society of Victoria are a not for profit animal welfare organisation committed to promoting, protecting and improving the wellbeing and welfare of all cats and kittens and rely solely on the amazing support of our generous members and customers.

With your support, we can continue to provide the highest level of care and comfort to cats and kittens in need and educate our local community on responsible cat ownership.
