title:  How Big My Email Marketing List Should Be?
date:   2018-1-19
---

Size of your email list doesn't matter. What matters is the engagement which your subscribers are making with your email newsletters that you send to them. If your list is having 10,000 subscribers but only 1% of them are engaging with your emails then that's very bad, in my opinion. On the other hand, if you have only 500 subscribers but more than 400 people buy your products or services when you send them any promotional email, then that's really nice.

When you have a bigger list you have to pay more to the newsletter service which you are using. Now, if that list is not engaging with your newsletter campaigns, then you are just wasting that money.

So, its better to have a small list but a active and engaging one.
